Children's Ministry

The Children's Ministry at our church is committed to reaching children with the gospel of Jesus Christ and teaching them to follow Him. We believe that every child is a gift from God, and we are committed to helping them grow in their faith and learn about the love of Jesus. We offer a variety of programs and services for children, including:

  • Sunday school
  • Vacation Bible school
  • After-school programs
  • Mentorship programs
  • Sports leagues

We believe that every child deserves to know the love of Jesus, and we are committed to making that happen. If you are interested in getting involved in our Children's Ministry, please contact us today.
