The Power of the Cross and Forgiveness

Speaker
Richard Okello Taban
Time &Date
July, 2023
Location
Juba, South Sudan

Today, I want to talk to you about the power of the cross and forgiveness. The cross is a symbol of pain, suffering, and death. But it is also a symbol of hope, redemption, and forgiveness. On the cross, Jesus Christ took the punishment for our sins. He bore the weight of our guilt and shame so that we could be free. When we forgive someone, we are not saying that what they did was okay. We are not saying that we don't feel hurt or angry. But we are saying that we are choosing to let go of the bitterness and resentment that is holding us back. Forgiveness is not easy. It takes courage and strength. But it is the only way to truly heal from the pain of the past. When we forgive, we are not just releasing the other person. We are also releasing ourselves. We are freeing ourselves from the burden of anger and bitterness. Forgiveness is a powerful act. It can heal our relationships, it can improve our mental health, and it can even extend our lifespan. So if you are holding on to anger or bitterness, I encourage you to let it go. Forgive the person who hurt you. Forgive yourself for your own mistakes. Forgiveness is not about forgetting what happened. It is about letting go of the pain and moving on. The cross is a symbol of forgiveness. It is a symbol of hope and new beginnings. So if you are struggling to forgive, I encourage you to look to the cross. Let the power of the cross wash over you and give you the strength to let go of the past. Thank you.

In addition to the power of forgiveness, the cross also represents the following:

  • The love of God
  • The victory of good over evil
  • The hope of eternal life
If you are struggling to forgive someone, I encourage you to pray to God for help. He is the ultimate source of forgiveness, and He will give you the strength to let go of the past and move on.
