Our Story

Our Church Story

The founder of the LWGC recieved the vision to fo ministry since the year 2000 and started by prayers and fastings, doing one-one evangelism and discipleship to the converts. It was not until 2014 that the Lord gave hime the burden to do a home cell fellowship consisting him and his wife and daughter. With no time families in the neighborhoods joined in and the fellowship grew bigger. During this time God let him visit homes of those in need of prayers and Deliverance and God added members daily to the ministry. A Sunday service was started and the church grew in the Word and faith. The grace of God and his presence gave the assurance and guarantee to take the gospel forward that led to the opening of branches of the church within and without the country.

WE EXIST TO:
  • To present to the lost the gospel message of salvation based on faith in the shed blood of Jesus Christ and a true repentance and conversion
  • To teach and nurture the believers toward a sanctified life and to faithfulness by God's grace and through His Word
  • To evangelise communities and nations by boldly taking the gospel to unreached areas
  • To show love to the needy through charitable services to fellow believers and nonbelievers

OUR  BELIEFS:
  • PASSION FOR GOD: We love God because he first loves us and taught us to love others.
  • THE SCRIPTURES: We believe the Bible is God’s inspired word, the authoritative and trustworthy rule of faith and practice for Christians
  • PRAYER: We believe that our church is, and should be a house of prayer (Matt. 21:13)
  • LOST PEOPLE: We believe that lost and unchurched people matter to God, therefore, it matters to our church. We value all people because all people need grace and forgiveness for sin, especially the lost
  • FELLOWSHIP: We Believe that fellowship among our people is a replica of our relationship with God; refreshing, encouraging, and a promotion of unity
  • INTEGRITY: We believe that after being born again God expects us to live a life of high standards and have integrity

Vision of the church

To seek those who are lost in the world and bring them back to God by sharing with them the gospel of Jesus Christ

Mission of the church

We exist to establish responsible disciples through teaching of sound doctrine, relying on the leading of the Holy Spirit and impacting the communities through changed lives
